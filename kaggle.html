Job Salaries Prediction

AICVS held another extremely exciting Kaggle competition centered on job salary prediction. The goal of this competition was to create a prediction model that can accurately forecast the salaries of employees in the data science domain using regression techniques or any other suitable algorithms. Participants were provided with a CSV data file containing relevant parameters such as job roles, qualifications, and experience, which they used to build their models. The competition took place between 10th November, 2022 and 17th November, 2022, and saw a total of 60 registrations. As a testament to the competitive nature of the event, only three winners emerged, showcasing their skills and expertise in the field of data science.

The winners of the competition were

  • Parnavi Kulkarni, Btech
  • Rajasi Barapatre, SY
  • Esha Chandaliya, TY

The participants who made submissions were given a Completion Certificate and the winners got a Certificate of Excellence.

We would like to thank the participants for showing so much vigor and enthusiasm for the Kaggle competitions hosted by us. We hope to bring you many more competitions like this.
